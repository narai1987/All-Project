Welcome to the pride of the Andaman sea! The MV Deep Andaman Queen, new refurbished in the year of 2012, is a 28.5-meters long and 7-meters wide, twin-engine steel hull motor boat, with the highest safety standards on board. She will be operating along Thailand's western coast and offering incredible diving safaris to locations that include Similan , Surin national marine parks and Richelieu Rock along with exclusive safaris to the little explored Mergui archipelago in Burma. Since November until the end of April 2012. From May until August, she is ready for charter rent in the area of Andaman southern sea, offering the chance to enjoy the incredible diving on offer in areas such as Koh Haa and Phi Phi along with the world class diving at Hin Daeng and Hin Muang and the coral gardens of Tarutao National Marine Park. All cabins onboard MV Deep Andaman Queen have air conditioning, en-suite bathroom and toilet, warm water shower, We have a variety of cabins configuration to suit our guests varied needs, from standard quad, triple and twin share cabins on the lower deck, Deluxe Double bed and Deluxe Twin bed cabins on the main deck. Not forgetting the pride of the vessel, our Master cabin, which boasts the best panoramic view any liveaboard master cabin can offer. MV Deep Andaman Queen also offers our guests one of the largest and most comfortable dive platforms in South East Asia; designed for divers.


MV Deep Andaman Queen is a PADI Dive Center that offers the Enriched Air (Nitrox) Diver course, Advanced Open Water course and many specialties. You can feel confident that our experienced dive crew can take care of your every diving need, whether you are a seasoned pro or a brand new first time diver! MV Deep Andaman Queen also carries a Nitrox membrane system onboard; we can fill cylinders with a mixture up to 32% Oxygen. Our Professional PADI Dive Instructors are certified gas blenders to ensure your safety. One of the pleasures of diving with MV Deep Andaman Queen is that you don’t have to worry about long surface swims after a dive! We will ensure you are picked up safely and quickly by one of our two dinghies or by our big boat. Once safely back on the boat, the crew will assist you with your dive equipment and offer you a cool drink. Enjoy our delicious food after the dives and continue with the relaxing schedule of the trips.

MV Deep Andaman Queen’s Similan-Surin-Richelieu Example Trip Itinerary Similan Island, Koh Bon, Koh Tachai, Richelieu Rock and Surin Island 5 Days / 4 Nights (14 Dives).

Package Includes

Accommodation on board, All meals, Snacks + Fruits, Towel, Tanks + Weight, Dive guide, Land transfer

Package Excludes

Hotel Stay, Equipment rental, Soft drinks + Alcohol, Airfare, Marine park tax (1600 baht), insurance, Nitrox refills

03:00 pm - 06:00 pm Pick-up at your Hotel, Phuket Airport, Dive shops & Khao Lak
(Pick-up times are according to the location)
06:00 pm - 08:00 pm Arrival at MV Deep Andaman Queen’s, (Tablamu pier)
Welcome Drinks - Cabin Allocation - Staff Introduction
Boat Briefing - Welcome Dinner

Day 2

07:00 am - 07:30 am General Diving Rules / Safety Briefing
07:00 am - 07:30 am Three day dives around the Similan Island
Visit Island No. 4 / Enjoy paradise with a beautiful white sandy beach.

The above intinerary is an example of and average cruise.
The Captain and Cruise leader will decide the best course of action based on weather condition at the specific time of each trip.
